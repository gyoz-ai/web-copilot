### English (en)

**Stop clicking. Start asking.**

gyoza is an AI copilot that lives in your browser. Tell it what you want — it reads the page, clicks buttons, fills forms, navigates between pages, and gets things done. On any website. No setup needed.

**What can gyoza do?**

- "Go to Amazon Japan and find me a meat strainer under ¥1,000" → navigates, searches, finds products
- "Fill out this form with my info and submit it" → reads form fields, fills them, submits
- "Translate this page to English" → translates all visible text in place
- "Add this item to my cart" → clicks the right buttons, handles popups and options
- "What's my account balance?" → reads the page and answers your question
- "Compare these 3 products" → extracts data and gives you a summary

**Works on ANY website instantly.** No special integration needed. gyoza reads the page HTML, understands the layout, and figures out how to help. For popular sites, community "recipes" make it even faster and more accurate.

**You choose the AI.** Bring your own API key for Claude (Anthropic), GPT (OpenAI), or Gemini (Google). Switch anytime. Or use our managed plan — no API key needed.

**Privacy first.** In BYOK mode, your API keys stay in your browser. Page content is only sent to the LLM you choose. Nothing stored on our servers.

**Key features:**

- Works on every website — no setup, no configuration
- Full browser control: navigate, click, fill forms, extract data, translate
- Multi-turn conversations with memory — handles complex multi-step tasks
- Smart verification: checks if actions actually worked before reporting success
- 30 languages supported in the interface
- Keyboard shortcut: Cmd+Shift+E (Mac) / Ctrl+Shift+E (Windows) to toggle
- Dark theme, customizable avatar, adjustable bubble opacity
- Conversation history — resume past chats anytime

**For developers:** Publish "recipes" (structured site descriptions) that make gyoza work even better on your website. Join the recipe marketplace at gyoz.ai.
